ABOUT US

WHO WE ARE


There’s nothing cookie-cutter about Pizza Hub. Not our pizzas. Not our people. And definitely not the way we
live life. Around here, we don’t settle for anything less than food we’re proud to serve. And we don’t just clock
in. Not when we can also become our best, make friends, and have fun while we’re at it. We’re the pizza
company that lives life unboxed.



WHERE WE COME FROM


We came from Ambur a small town in TamilNadu. Our First Shop Open at Ambur in 2020 with a Investment of 2 Lakhs
and We Named it Pizza Hub. How profound! Soon, the restaurant grew. Why? The pizza was awesome. The service
felt like home. And the customers were treated like family. And we’ve been delivering that same food and service ever since.



Pizza Hub


Our restaurant brand offers an exciting menu consisting of its signature pizzas. We create food we’re proud to serve

We offer full-service catering for any event, large or small. We understand your needs and we will cater the food to satisfy the biggerst criteria of them all, both look and taste.
